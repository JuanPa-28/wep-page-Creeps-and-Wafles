1. Leadership and Talent Management

Problem: Centralized or authoritarian leadership style

Strategy: Encourage participative leadership through regular team meetings where employees can voice opinions and contribute to decisions.

Problem: Lack of performance recognition

Strategy: Implement an employee recognition program with monthly awards, performance bonuses, or public acknowledgment during team briefings.

Problem: High staff turnover

Strategy: Conduct regular satisfaction surveys and exit interviews to understand causes and improve working conditions, career growth opportunities, and staff engagement.

2. Internal Problems

Strategy 1: Implement a digital ordering system with a user-friendly app or touchscreen kiosks to streamline ordering and reduce wait times.

Strategy 2: Assign specific staff to table turnover. Create a rotation schedule and train staff to manage cleaning and table prep efficiently.

Strategy 3: Optimize table layout. Use space planning software to redesign layouts and test during off-peak hours to evaluate efficiency.

Strategy 4: Offer complementary items like free samples (cookies, mini drinks) to improve customer experience during long waits.

3. Strategic and Business Problems

Strategy: Franchise system. Develop a franchise kit with branding, training, and operational guidelines to expand locations while maintaining quality.

Strategy: Marketing investment. Allocate budget for seasonal campaigns, influencer collaborations, and local advertising.

Strategy: Environmental awareness. Launch a sustainability campaign highlighting eco-friendly practices and customer participation (e.g., discounts for reusable containers).

Strategy: Promotions. Offer weekday deals, combo meals, or limited-time offers promoted through social media and email marketing.

4. Technology and Service Issues

Strategy 1: Assign a social media manager to handle content, respond to messages, and track engagement metrics.

Strategy 2: Provide WiFi password after purchase by integrating it into digital or paper receipts.

Strategy 3: Create an in-house delivery team with GPS tracking to improve delivery times and customer satisfaction.

5. Financial Problems

Strategy: Build relationships with local producers for discounts. Adjust menu prices gradually and introduce high-margin combo meals. Monitor inventory to minimize food waste.

6. Competition and Customer Problems

Strategy 1: Improve service with training focused on speed, friendliness, and professionalism.

Strategy 2: Differentiate with rotating menu items, themed nights, or customer engagement events.

Strategy 3: Implement a digital loyalty program using QR codes or apps where customers earn points redeemable for rewards.
